About the Course

The Fundamental Analysis Bootcamp is a beginner-friendly, hands-on journey into the world of stock market investing. Over 3 weeks, participants learn to decode financial statements, understand valuation metrics, assess a company’s true worth, identify the right stocks to invest in, and build basic financial models using the DCF (Discounted Cash Flow) method; making them confident and self-sufficient investors by the end of the course.
